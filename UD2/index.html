Insect

The    insects(scientific name Insecta Linnaeus , 1758 ) are a class of invertebrate animals , and the largest group within the phylum of arthropods , characterized by having a pair of antennae , three pairs of legs and two pairs of wings (which, however , may be reduced or missing). The science that studies insects is called entomology. more information
